  1. Neck to Shoulder: Measure from the middle of the neck to end of shoulder (where the sleeve starts).
  2. Sleeve length (A): Measure from the center back of collar to the end of shoulder.
  3. Sleeve length (B): Measure from the shoulder to the wrist.
  4. Shoulder to Shoulder: Measure this length from one shoulder to another.
  5. Chest: Measure around the fullest part, place the tape close under the arms and make sure the tape is flat across the back.
  6. Front Length: Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the desired hemline.
  7. Hem: Measure from one side to another, but it is vital to take this measurement from above the elastic area (If sweatshirt have any).
  8. Sleeve cuff: At the widest point of the wrist, measure its circumference. Leave enough room for watches or jewelry worn often. Take this measurement from above the elastic area same as Hem length measurement

The Blvck Classic Shirt redefines everyday sophistication with a sharply tailored profile and minimalist construction. Designed for those who favor clean lines and quiet power, this long-sleeve button-up is a versatile staple crafted for refined dressing and elevated layering.

Key Features:

  • Premium Fabrication: Cut from a smooth, structured fabric with a matte finish that blends softness with durability—perfect for both formal and off-duty wear.

  • Minimalist Silhouette: Designed with precision—features a traditional collar, clean front placket, and a straight hem that tucks seamlessly or drapes effortlessly.

  • Functional Details: One front chest pocket adds subtle utility, while tonal Blvck embroidery on the pocket underscores the shirt’s stealth aesthetic.

  • Tailored Fit: Cut to offer a crisp, contemporary silhouette without compromising on comfort—slightly structured through the shoulders with a relaxed drape through the body.

  • Monochrome Finish: Rendered in true deep black for a timeless, seasonless look that pairs with anything in the BLVCK wardrobe.

Style Suggestions:

Wear it solo for a clean minimalist look, layer over a tee for street-style ease, or pair with the Blvck Classic Pants for a complete monochrome uniform.
